Quality Control (QC)

Ensuring Purity, Precision & Compliance in Every Raw Material We Deliver

At MYRTACE HEALTHCARE PRIVATE LIMITED, Quality Control is the backbone of our pharmaceutical raw material manufacturing process. We follow stringent testing, documentation, and compliance standards to ensure that every batch of raw materials meets global pharmacopoeial requirements and exceeds customer expectations.

Our QC operations are aligned with:

  • GMP / cGMP guidelines
  • Pharmacopoeias: IP, BP, USP, EP
  • ICH Q2, Q3, Q7 standards
  • ISO quality systems
  • Regulatory requirements of domestic & international markets

Our QC Capabilities

1. Advanced Analytical Laboratories

  • Fully equipped laboratories for HPLC, GC, UV-Visible Spectrophotometry, FTIR, Karl Fischer Titration, Polarimetry
  • Analytical Balances, pH & Conductivity Meters, Melting Point & Loss on Drying (LOD) systems
  • Heavy metal & impurity testing instruments

We continuously upgrade our analytical capabilities to meet the evolving needs of the pharmaceutical industry.

Testing & Analysis We Perform

  • Raw Materials: Identity verification, purity analysis, moisture content, foreign matter & impurities testing, residual solvent analysis, microbiological screening
  • Intermediates / In-Process Materials: Monitoring of critical process parameters, assay & impurity profiling, physical parameters, ensuring product consistency
  • Finished Raw Material / API / Excipients: Assay (active content), related substances, stability and degradation checks, particle size analysis, microbial contamination testing, compliance with pharmacopeial standards

Documentation & Traceability

  • Certificate of Analysis (COA)
  • Batch Manufacturing Records (BMR)
  • Batch Packaging Records (BPR)
  • Standard Test Procedures (STPs)
  • Method Validation Reports
  • Calibration Records
  • Change Control, Deviations & CAPA documentation
  • All documentation follows Good Documentation Practices (GDP)

Quality Assurance Support

  • Validate analytical methods
  • Maintain stability programs
  • Conduct internal audits and cross-checks
  • Ensure regulatory readiness
  • Release batches only after complete QC & QA approval

Controlled Laboratory Environment

  • Segregated QC labs
  • Controlled temperature & humidity zones
  • Clean sampling rooms
  • Protected storage areas for retained samples

Packaging & Storage Compliance

  • Protection from contamination
  • Correct labeling
  • Compliance with pharmacopeial packaging norms
  • FEFO & FIFO inventory practices

Customer-Centric Quality Approach

  • Custom testing support as per client requirements
  • Quick turnaround time for QC approvals
  • Transparent reporting
  • Batch-to-batch consistency
  • Regulatory-ready documentation for domestic & export markets

Certifications & Compliance (as applicable)

  • ISO 9001:2015
  • WHO-GMP
  • cGMP compliance
  • GLP certification
  • Regulatory audits by clients & authorities
