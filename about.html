About Us

  • Myrtace Healthcare Private Limited is a start-up research organization founded by a team of young professionals & technologists who have 15+ years of research experience in chemical and pharmaceutical industry.
  • Our leadership team is expert in designing and synthesizing complex, difficult-to-synthesize compounds using cutting-edge technologies and delivers projects within committed timelines.
  • We have a team of Synthetic Organic Chemists with in-depth knowledge and experience in multi-step organic synthesis.
  • Our well-trained Process Chemists can work in Process R&D labs, pilot plants and supervise production activities when required.

What we offer?

  • Custom Synthesis of New Chemical Entities (NCEs) and pharmaceutical products (from mg to Kg scale) as per the requirements and specifications of clients’ requirements.
  • Process Development of APIs and Advanced Intermediates that are cost effective scalable to commercial scale (Tech transfer available)
  • Contract Research and Manufacturing of Fine Organic Chemicals and Specialty Chemicals (from Kg scale to bulk scale)
    • Synthesis of impurity standards (from mg to gram scale)
  • Synthesis of metabolites and degradation impurities, isotope labelled compounds etc. (from mg to gram scale)
  • Isolation and characterization of unknown impurities

With 15 years of excellence, we deliver certified pharma raw materials to manufacturing units, ensuring purity, consistency and on-time supply.
