Career Opportunities

1. Quality Control (QC) Chemist

  • Qualifications: B.Sc / M.Sc in Chemistry, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, or related field
  • Experience: 0–3 years (entry), 3–7 years (senior)
  • Responsibilities: Chemical and instrumental analysis, stability studies, COA preparation, adherence to GLP/GMP, investigation of deviations

2. Quality Assurance (QA) Executive

  • Qualifications: B.Pharm / M.Pharm / M.Sc (Chemistry/Pharma)
  • Experience: 1–5 years
  • Responsibilities: SOP/BMR/BPR management, change control, deviations, CAPA, validation, audits, GMP compliance, batch release

3. Production / Manufacturing Officer

  • Qualifications: B.Pharm / M.Pharm / B.Sc (Chemistry)
  • Experience: 0–5 years
  • Responsibilities: Operate and monitor FBD, RMG, tablet press, coating machines, batch execution, coordinate with QA/QC, maintain records

4. Regulatory Affairs (RA) Officer

  • Qualifications: B.Pharm / M.Pharm (Regulatory Affairs preferred)
  • Experience: 1–4 years
  • Responsibilities: Prepare/review CTD/eCTD dossiers, product registrations, renewals, variations, regulatory liaison, labeling compliance

5. Research & Development (R&D) Chemist

  • Qualifications: M.Sc (Chemistry) / M.Pharm (Pharmaceutics / Pharmaceutical Chemistry)
  • Experience: 1–6 years
  • Responsibilities: Formulation development, pre-formulation studies, analytical method development, technical reporting, stability & scale-up batches

6. Microbiologist

  • Qualifications: B.Sc / M.Sc in Microbiology
  • Experience: 0–3 years
  • Responsibilities: Microbial testing, sterility/endotoxin tests, water & environment monitoring, disinfectant validation, documentation

7. Packaging Development Executive

  • Qualifications: B.Pharm / M.Pharm / B.Tech (Packaging Technology)
  • Experience: 1–4 years
  • Responsibilities: Packaging material selection, vendor coordination, artwork/labeling, compatibility trials, cost-effective solutions

8. Machine Operator / Technician

  • Qualifications: ITI / Diploma (Mechanical/Electrical) or 12th pass with experience
  • Experience: 1–5 years
  • Responsibilities: Operate blister/strip/tablet press/labeling/filling machines, routine maintenance, BMIs, hygiene and safety compliance

9. Sales & Marketing (Pharma Representative)

  • Qualifications: Any graduate (Science/Pharma preferred)
  • Experience: 0–3 years
  • Responsibilities: Promote products to doctors/hospitals, achieve sales targets, competitor tracking, CMEs, promotional events

10. HR & Admin Executive (Pharma Experience Preferred)

  • Qualifications: MBA HR / BBA / Graduate with HR experience
  • Experience: 1–4 years
  • Responsibilities: Recruitment, attendance, payroll, training, documentation, audits, admin coordination

11. Store & Inventory Executive

  • Qualifications: Graduate (Science preferred)
  • Experience: 1–3 years
  • Responsibilities: Material receipt, inspection, storage, stock maintenance, coordinate QA, issue materials, FEFO/FIFO compliance

Internship Program

At Myrtace Healthcare Pvt Ltd, our Internship Program is designed to give students and research fellows real-world exposure to pharmaceutical manufacturing, quality processes, and research activities. Interns work closely with experienced professionals and gain valuable industry skills that support their academic and career growth.

What We Offer

  • Hands-on training in QC, QA, Production, and R&D
  • Guidance from industry experts
  • Opportunity to work on live assignments and technical documentation
  • Exposure to GMP, safety practices, and regulatory workflows

Who Can Apply

Students pursuing degrees in Pharmacy, Chemical Engineering, Biotechnology, Chemistry, or related fields.

Education Required

  • B.Pharm / M.Pharm
  • B.Sc / M.Sc (Chemistry, Biochemistry, Microbiology)
  • B.E / B.Tech (Chemical Engineering, Biotechnology)
  • Diploma in Chemical / Pharmacy streams

Age Limit

  • Minimum: 18 years
  • Maximum: 28 years (exceptions may be considered based on academic programs)

Benefits

  • Practical industrial experience
  • Certificate upon successful completion
  • Opportunity for future employment based on performance

How to Apply

Send your resume and department preference to info@myrtace.com with the subject “Internship Application – Your Name”.
