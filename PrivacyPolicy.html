Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Myrtace Healthcare Pvt Ltd collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data obtained through the website. By using this website, users agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, Email address, Phone number, Postal address, Job application data (resume, qualifications, employment history), and any information voluntarily submitted through forms.
  • Non-Personal / Technical Information: IP address, Browser type, Device information, Cookies and tracking data, Website navigation patterns, Approximate location.
  • Sensitive Information (If Applicable): Health-related queries submitted voluntarily, Feedback related to product experience. (We do not collect sensitive health data without user consent.)

3. How We Use the Information

  • Respond to inquiries or requests
  • Send newsletters, updates, or alerts (only with consent)
  • Process job applications
  • Provide product information permitted by law
  • Enhance website functionality and user experience
  • Conduct research, analytics, and service improvements
  • Regulatory or safety reporting, if required
  • Prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activities

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • User consent
  • Legitimate business interests
  • Compliance with pharmaceutical regulatory obligations
  • Contractual requirements (where applicable)

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

The website uses cookies for personalization, performance optimization, and analytics. Users may disable cookies via browser settings—but some website features may not function properly.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent personal information. Data may be shared only with:

  • Authorized employees or departments
  • Regulatory authorities (for pharmacovigilance, safety reporting, audits)
  • Service providers (IT support, analytics, email services)
  • Partners involved in clinical research or collaborations (as legally permitted)

All third parties are obligated to follow strict data protection rules.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures (encryption, firewalls, access control). Although strong protective measures are applied, no digital system is 100% risk-free.

8. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for the duration needed to fulfill its purpose or comply with legal requirements. Job application data is kept for future opportunities only if the candidate provides consent.

9. Children’s Privacy

The website is not intended for individuals under 18 years. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. International Transfers

If data is transferred outside the user’s country, safeguards compliant with global data protection laws (GDPR, HIPAA, local regulations) will be applied.

11. User Rights

  • Access their data
  • Correct or update information
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Object to data use for marketing
  • Request a copy of personal data (data portability)

Requests can be made through info@myrtace.com

12. Updates to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with the revised date.
