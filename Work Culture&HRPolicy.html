Work Culture & HR Policy

Our Work Culture

At Myrtace Healthcare Pvt Ltd, we believe that people are the driving force behind our scientific excellence and industry leadership. Our work environment is built on integrity, collaboration, innovation, and respect—ensuring every team member feels valued, supported, and inspired to deliver their best.

Key Pillars of Our Culture

  • Integrity & Accountability: Upholding the highest ethical standards. Employees are encouraged to take responsibility and maintain transparency.
  • Innovation-Driven Mindset: Encouraging curiosity, experimentation, and scientific thinking for safer, efficient, high-quality products.
  • Teamwork & Collaboration: Cross-functional teams work together, combining QC, QA, R&D, production, logistics, and regulatory expertise.
  • Respect & Inclusion: Promoting diversity, mutual respect, and an inclusive environment.
  • Safety & Compliance First: Employees follow strict cGMP, regulatory, and EHS guidelines at all times.

HR Policies

1. Equal Opportunity Employment

We provide equal opportunities regardless of gender, age, background, or beliefs. Hiring, promotions, and evaluations are strictly based on skills, performance, and organizational needs.

2. Employee Onboarding & Training

  • Structured onboarding including company orientation, safety & GMP training, role-specific technical training, and policy awareness.
  • Continuous learning programs to stay updated with industry practices and regulatory requirements.

3. Performance Management & Growth

  • Transparent performance evaluation promoting improvement and career growth.
  • Clear goal-setting, periodic feedback, skill development opportunities, and internal promotions.

4. Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) Compliance

  • Safe working conditions, regular health check-ups, safety drills, and EHS training.
  • Strict adherence to GxP, ISO, and regulatory standards.

5. Code of Conduct

  • No tolerance for harassment or discrimination.
  • Confidentiality of company information.
  • Responsible use of company property.
  • Honest and transparent communication.

6. Leave & Attendance Policy

  • Earned/privilege leave, casual leave, medical leave, maternity/paternity benefits.
  • Festival and national holidays.
  • Flexible attendance policies supporting work-life balance.

7. Reward & Recognition

  • Monthly and annual awards, spot recognition, incentives for exceptional contributions.
  • Appreciation for long-term service.

8. Grievance Redressal

  • Defined, confidential mechanism for raising concerns.
  • HR ensures quick action, transparency, and fair resolution.

9. Open-Door Communication

Employees are encouraged to share ideas, suggestions, or concerns directly with supervisors or management.

10. Employee Engagement & Welfare

  • Wellness programs, team building activities, cultural celebrations, skill development sessions, CSR initiatives.

Additional HR Policies

  • Recruitment & Selection Policy: Candidate screening for qualifications, GMP awareness, background checks, and medical fitness.
  • Training & Competency Policy: Mandatory GMP/GDP training at induction and regular intervals; role-specific SOP training.
  • Code of Conduct / Ethics Policy: Promotes ethical behavior, data integrity (ALCOA+), and zero tolerance for falsification, harassment, or misconduct.
  • Attendance & Shift Policy: Work hours, overtime rules, shift rotations, and fatigue management.
  • Leave Policy: Annual, sick, casual, and training leave, including quarantine/isolation leave.
  • Gowning & Hygiene Policy: Grooming standards, hygiene practices, gowning SOP adherence for cleanroom staff.
  • Performance Management Policy: Goal-based evaluations aligned with quality, productivity, and compliance.
  • Disciplinary Policy: Handling deviations, misconduct, or non-compliance (verbal warnings, CAPAs, suspension, termination).
  • Health & Safety Policy: Workplace safety, PPE usage, ergonomics, and emergency handling.
  • Data Privacy & Security Policy: Compliance with HIPAA/GDPR-type standards, protecting employee and patient information.
  • Whistleblower & Ethics Policy: Protected reporting channels for unethical behavior or compliance breaches.
  • Confidentiality & Non-Disclosure: Protection of company information and trade secrets.
  • Employee Conduct: Expected behavior and workplace standards.
  • Employee Exit Policy: Knowledge transfer, QA/QC clearance, and exit interviews.

These policies ensure GMP compliance, minimize human error, promote safety, and support regulatory audits.

Conclusion

At Myrtace Healthcare Pvt Ltd, we are committed to creating a workplace where employees thrive, grow, and feel proud of their contributions. Our HR policies and work culture are designed to support both individual development and organizational excellence—ensuring a safe, ethical, and fulfilling environment for all.

With 15 years of excellence, we deliver certified pharma raw materials to manufacturing units, ensuring purity, consistency and on-time supply.
